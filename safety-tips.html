Safety Tips

Your safety is our priority. Please follow these tips to ensure a secure and pleasant experience when buying or selling on Ghana Market.

For Buyers

  • Meet in Public: Always arrange to meet sellers in a safe, public place like a coffee shop, shopping mall, or police station. Avoid secluded areas.
  • Inspect Before Paying: Thoroughly check the item to ensure it matches the description and is in working order before you hand over any money.
  • Never Pay in Advance: Do not send money for deposits or pre-payments. All transactions should be completed in person at the time of exchange.
  • Bring a Friend: If possible, take a friend or family member with you, especially if you are buying a high-value item.
  • Trust Your Gut: If a deal seems too good to be true, or if the seller is behaving suspiciously, it's okay to walk away.

For Sellers

  • Verify Payment: When accepting cash, check for counterfeit notes. If using Mobile Money, ensure you have received the funds in your account before handing over the item.
  • Choose a Safe Location: Just like buyers, sellers should insist on meeting in a well-lit, public area.
  • Protect Your Information: Only share necessary information. There's no need to share your home address or other sensitive personal details.
  • Be Wary of Scams: Be cautious of buyers who offer more than the asking price, ask you to ship the item before payment, or use complex payment methods.
