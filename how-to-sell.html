How to Sell on Ghana Market

Selling your items on Ghana Market is simple and fast. Follow these steps to get started and reach thousands of potential buyers across the country.

Step 1: Create Your Account

If you don't have an account, click on "Login / Register" on the homepage and choose the "Register" option. Fill in your details to create your free account.

Step 2: Get Listing Credits

To list an item for sale, you need at least one "Listing Credit". After registering, you can import credits that you have purchased from an admin. Click the "Import" button in the header and paste the credit token you received.

Step 3: Click "Sell Item"

Once you are logged in and have credits, the "+ Sell Item" button will appear in the navigation bar. Click it to open the selling form.

Step 4: Fill in Item Details

Provide a clear and descriptive title, set a fair price, and write an honest description of your item. Be sure to select the correct category and provide your location and WhatsApp number so buyers can contact you.

Step 5: Upload Photos

A picture is worth a thousand cedis! Upload clear, high-quality photos of your item from different angles. You can upload files from your device or take a photo directly with your camera.

Step 6: Submit Your Listing

Once you've filled out all the details, click the "List Item" button at the bottom. One listing credit will be deducted, and your item will instantly go live on the marketplace. Now, just wait for interested buyers to contact you via WhatsApp!
